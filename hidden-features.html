The Cuyler House

Hidden Features

Abolitionists used a diversity of tactics to achieve their goals. Among those tactics were those of subterfuge: hiding in plain sight, falsifying documents, even building homes from the ground-up to provide hidden spaces unknown to the state and difficult to detect by an invading force without close scrutiny.

The Cuyler House was one such house.
